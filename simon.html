Neon Simon – Memory Sequence Pattern Game

Follow the Neon Lights

Neon Simon challenges your memory by playing a sequence of glowing colors. Your task is to repeat the exact order. Each successful round extends the sequence by one, quickly turning the challenge into a true memory endurance test.

How to Play

Watch the lights flash. Then tap the squares in the same order to repeat the pattern. One mistake ends the game.

Controls

Tap the glowing pads in sequence.

Tips

  • Say the color names out loud to enhance retention.
  • Break long sequences into smaller chunks mentally.
