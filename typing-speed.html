Typing Speed – Neon Typing Accuracy & WPM Tester

How Fast Can You Type?

Typing Speed is a neon-themed typing test that measures how many words per minute (WPM) you can type while maintaining accuracy. Perfect for students, professionals, and gamers who want to track and improve typing ability.

How to Play

Type the text shown on screen as quickly and accurately as possible. Your WPM score is calculated based on speed and mistakes.

Tips

  • Focus on accuracy first — speed will follow.
  • Use all fingers for maximum efficiency.
  • Keep your eyes on the text, not the keyboard.
