Crack the Glowing Security Grid

Pattern Lock reimagines the classic connect-the-dots unlocking system found on digital devices. Your task is to trace a glowing pattern across nodes in the exact sequence shown, replicating security locks found in futuristic neon interfaces. With each level, patterns grow longer, more complex, and harder to memorize.

How to Play

A pattern preview appears briefly. Memorize the order, then draw the exact sequence by connecting the neon nodes in one continuous gesture.

Controls

Desktop: Click and drag over nodes.

Mobile: Swipe continuously over the dots.

Tips

  • Focus on the pattern shape rather than individual points.
  • Start slow until your muscle memory improves.
  • Break long patterns into smaller mental segments.

Developer Insights

This game uses gesture tracking for smooth, accurate pattern recognition, ensuring mobile users get a natural lock-screen-style experience.
