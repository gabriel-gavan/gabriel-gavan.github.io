Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data may be processed when you use Neon Mini Game Hub. We aim to collect as little as possible while keeping the site functional.

⬅ Back to Hub Articles About Terms Contact

1) What this site is

Neon Mini Game Hub is a browser-based game hub and article site. You can play most games without creating an account.

2) Data we may process

2.1 Server logs

Hosting/CDN providers may process basic technical logs to deliver the service and protect it from abuse.

2.2 Local storage (on your device)

Some games may use localStorage to store settings or scores. This stays on your device.

2.3 Comments / feedback

If you post comments or homepage feedback, the text you submit is stored so it can be displayed to others.

  • the comment text
  • a display name you enter
  • a timestamp and ID to support replies
Do not post personal information. Comments are public.

3) Cookies, consent, and advertising

We may display ads via third-party providers. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies.

  • serve ads
  • measure performance
  • prevent fraud and abuse
  • (where allowed) personalize ads

4) Analytics

We may use analytics to understand what pages are popular and to improve performance.

5) Sharing and third parties

We do not sell your personal data. Some data may be processed by services we use to operate the site.

6) Children’s privacy

This site is intended for a general audience. Contact us if personal information is posted by mistake.

7) Your choices

  • Cookies: use consent/settings and browser controls.
  • Local storage: clear site data in browser settings.
  • Comments: contact us to request removal.

8) Contact

For privacy requests, email contact@neonminigamehub.com.
