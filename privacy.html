Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data may be processed when you use Neon Mini Game Hub. We aim to collect as little as possible while keeping the site functional.

⬅ Back to Hub Articles About Terms Contact

1) What this site is

Neon Mini Game Hub is a browser-based game hub and article site. You can play most games without creating an account.

2) Data we may process

2.1 Server logs

Hosting/CDN providers may process basic technical logs (e.g., IP address, user agent, requested URL, and timestamps) to deliver the service and protect it from abuse.

2.2 Local storage (on your device)

Some games may use localStorage to store settings or local scores. This stays on your device and can be cleared via your browser settings.

2.3 Comments / feedback

If you post comments or homepage feedback, the text you submit is stored so it can be displayed to other visitors. Depending on the implementation, we may store:

  • the comment text
  • a display name (username) you enter
  • a timestamp and an ID to support replies/threads
Do not post personal information. Comments are public.

3) Cookies, consent, and advertising

We may display ads via third-party ad providers (such as Google). These providers may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • serve ads
  • measure performance
  • prevent fraud and abuse
  • (where allowed) personalize ads

If you are in regions where consent is required, a consent banner/settings UI should be shown so you can choose your preferences.

4) Analytics

We may use analytics to understand what pages are popular and to improve performance. If analytics are enabled, they may set cookies or use similar identifiers depending on the provider and your consent choice.

5) Sharing and third parties

We do not sell your personal data. Some data may be processed by third-party services we use to operate the site (e.g., hosting/CDN, comments storage, ad providers). Those services process data under their own policies.

6) Children’s privacy

This site is intended for a general audience. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us via comments, contact us and we will remove it where feasible.

7) Your choices

  • Cookies: use the site’s consent/settings controls (if shown) and your browser controls.
  • Local storage: clear site data in your browser settings to reset local game settings/scores.
  • Comments: contact us to request removal of a comment you posted (include the page URL and the text/timestamp).

8) Contact

For privacy requests, email contact@neonminigamehub.com.
