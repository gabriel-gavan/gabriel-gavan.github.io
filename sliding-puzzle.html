Sliding Puzzle – Neon Tile Rearrangement Challenge

Rearrange the Neon Grid

Sliding Puzzle brings the timeless tile rearrangement challenge into a neon environment. Shift tiles around the empty slot to recreate the correct numerical order or complete a glowing image. It’s simple in concept, but increasingly difficult as the puzzles grow.

How to Play

Tap any tile adjacent to the empty space to slide it. Continue sliding until the puzzle is fully solved.

Tips

  • Focus on solving the top row first.
  • Use the final empty space strategically.
