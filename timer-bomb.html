Timer Bomb – Neon Countdown Puzzle Survival

Beat the Clock, Defuse the Bomb

Timer Bomb is a fast-paced challenge where you must solve micro-puzzles before the timer hits zero. With each success, the difficulty increases and the available time decreases.

How to Play

Complete the instructions shown on-screen — tap, match, solve, or react — before the bomb timer expires.

Tips

  • Stay calm — panic wastes precious seconds.
  • Expect the rules to change every round.
